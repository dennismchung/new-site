Student Assignments and Assessments

Kiddom focused on providing a unique mobile-first experience for the student taking an assignment or assessment in class.

Overview

A major school district of 5,000 students uses Kiddom and requested the use of our app in the classroom. Each student has a tablet, chromebook or smart phone (iOS/Android) for themselves. With this in mind we wanted to create a more intuitive way to take assessments and assignments than our web app.

To start our process we researched and found the most common types of questions teachers would ask on an assignment.

Students can answer assessment questions, and assignments that include:

  • Multiple choice
  • Open Ended - Attach a file to the answer from camera, voice, photos, icloud etc.
  • Short response -Compose a response using a textbox
